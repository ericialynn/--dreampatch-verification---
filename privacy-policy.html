Privacy Policy

DreamPatch Creator (“we”, “us”, or “our”) respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that data.

Information We Collect

We collect only the data necessary to authenticate users and enable content posting through TikTok’s Developer APIs. This may include:

  • TikTok user ID and access token (via Login Kit)
  • Content metadata such as video titles, captions, and thumbnails

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data solely to:

  • Authenticate your TikTok account
  • Enable content creation and posting via TikTok’s Content Posting API
  • Display your TikTok content using Display API

Data Storage and Security

We do not store personal data beyond session tokens required for TikTok integration. All data is processed securely and temporarily to complete your requested actions.

Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell, share, or disclose your data to third parties. All interactions are limited to TikTok’s platform and APIs.

Your Rights

You may revoke access to DreamPatch Creator at any time through your TikTok account settings. We do not retain any personal data once access is revoked.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of the app after changes means you accept the revised policy.
